# Failure Modes

## F19-1 — No Sleep / Over-Persistence

```text
coherence cost explodes
→ closure risk increases
```

## F19-2 — Excess Sleep / Under-Persistence

```text
Δϕ becomes too weak
→ meaning/value formation fails
```

## F19-3 — Corrupted Sleep

```text
maintenance fails
→ drift increases upon waking
```

## Diagnostic rule

Do not treat all persistence as good.

Do not treat all maintenance as collapse.

Maintenance must preserve future Δϕ availability.
