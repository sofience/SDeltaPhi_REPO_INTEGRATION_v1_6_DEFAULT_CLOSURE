# Minimal Definitions

## Δϕ Persistence

The continued availability of non-zero phase-change dynamics.

```text
Δϕ ↛ 0
```

## Consciousness

A functional regulator that maintains Δϕ persistence and prevents collapse into Δϕ → 0.

```text
Consciousness := Regulate(Δϕ) such that Δϕ ↛ 0.
```

## Sleep

A maintenance mode that reorganizes traces, reduces drift, and lowers future persistence cost.

```text
Sleep := Maintenance(Δϕ) by reorganizing traces and reducing drift.
```

## Coherence Cost

The cost required to maintain continued wake-state coherence.

```text
Cost_coherence
```

## Maintenance Cost

The cost required to enter a maintenance/reset mode.

```text
Cost_maintenance
```

## Mode Switch

The transition from wake regulation to sleep maintenance when coherence cost rises faster than maintenance/reset cost.

```text
Switch→Sleep ⇔ d(Cost_coherence)/dt > d(Cost_maintenance)/dt.
```

## Drift

Accumulating deviation in trace organization or transition stability.

## Dream

Optional low-cost rehearsal of transition candidates.

```text
Dream := low-cost rehearsal of transition candidates.
```
