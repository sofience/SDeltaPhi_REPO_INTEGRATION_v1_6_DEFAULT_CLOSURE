# Mode Switch Condition

A system switches to sleep when continued coherence becomes more costly than maintenance/reset.

```text
Switch→Sleep ⇔ d(Cost_coherence)/dt > d(Cost_maintenance)/dt.
```

## Interpretation

Wake regulation continues while coherence cost remains manageable.

Sleep becomes the lower-cost path when coherence cost rises faster than maintenance/reset cost.

## Diagnostic question

Is the system maintaining coherence at a rising cost when maintenance would reduce future drift?
