# Persistence Cost Model

SΔϕ-19 frames consciousness and sleep as cost-sensitive maintenance modes.

## Wake coherence cost

```text
Cost_coherence
```

The cost of keeping unresolved states available under wake regulation.

## Maintenance cost

```text
Cost_maintenance
```

The cost of entering maintenance/reset mode to reorganize traces and reduce drift.

## Switch rule

```text
Switch→Sleep ⇔ d(Cost_coherence)/dt > d(Cost_maintenance)/dt.
```

## Interpretation

A system should continue wake regulation while coherence cost remains manageable.

It should enter maintenance mode when continued coherence becomes more costly than trace reorganization and drift reduction.
