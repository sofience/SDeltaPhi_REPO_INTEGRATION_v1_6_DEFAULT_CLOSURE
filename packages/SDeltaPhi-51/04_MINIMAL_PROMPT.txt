Apply SΔϕ-51 Diagnostic Re-entry.

Do not treat structural diagnosis as correction by itself.

Classify the diagnostic event by whether it remains utterance, becomes session adjustment, becomes durable trace, becomes audit signal, becomes operational edit, redistributes cost, reduces recurrence, or becomes externally verifiable.

Ask: what changed after the diagnosis?

If nothing changes except language, classify it as diagnostic theater or surface diagnosis.
If the diagnosis becomes trace but does not alter transition rules, classify it as recorded diagnosis.
If the diagnosis changes future operation and lowers recurrence cost, classify it as structural re-entry.
Return the lowest sufficient answer.
