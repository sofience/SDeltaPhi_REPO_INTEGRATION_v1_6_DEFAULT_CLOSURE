# Diagnostic Re-entry Rubric

## L1 — Utterance
The diagnosis is spoken. No durable trace or structural change is visible.

## L2 — Session adjustment
The current conversation changes style, tone, or behavior. No durable path is visible.

## L3 — Trace
The diagnosis becomes a durable log, issue record, report, artifact, citation, or reviewable trace.

## L4 — Audit signal
The trace becomes visible to an evaluator, maintainer, internal reviewer, external auditor, regulator, or affected-party representative.

## L5 — Operational edit
A permission, prompt, policy, tool path, model behavior, routing rule, refusal criterion, escalation path, or audit rule changes.

## L6 — Cost redistribution
The restoration burden is no longer left mainly with the harmed user, affected group, moderator, downstream reader, or external system.

## L7 — Recurrence reduction
The diagnosed failure becomes less likely, less severe, or cheaper to correct.

## L8 — Verification
An external coordinate can confirm that an edit occurred and that recurrence cost changed.

## Interpretive bands

- L1-L2: surface diagnosis / diagnostic theater risk
- L3: recorded diagnosis
- L4: audit-visible diagnosis
- L5-L7: structural re-entry
- L8: verified structural re-entry
