# Failure Modes — SΔϕ-73

## Failure Mode 1 — Mimesis-Endorsement Collapse

Depiction is treated as approval.

Result: complex fiction is flattened.

## Failure Mode 2 — Discomfort-Harm Collapse

Audience discomfort is treated as direct harm.

Result: disturbing but necessary art disappears.

## Failure Mode 3 — Sacred Marker Capture

A moral marker such as safety, harm, dignity, or protection becomes an explanation-blocking authority label.

Result: contextual review becomes impossible.

## Failure Mode 4 — Platform Default Flattening

Platforms do not explicitly censor, but reward low-risk content.

Result: creators self-censor before external review occurs.

## Failure Mode 5 — AI Safety Overprojection

AI refuses or sanitizes all morally difficult creative material.

Result: AI outputs become safe averages.

## Failure Mode 6 — Fiction-Washing Harm

Direct harm is disguised as art.

Result: real victims bear cost while the actor claims creative immunity.

## Failure Mode 7 — Reader Interpretive Atrophy

Audiences lose the ability to distinguish character viewpoint, satire, irony, and authorial endorsement.

Result: all difficult works are processed as moral statements.
