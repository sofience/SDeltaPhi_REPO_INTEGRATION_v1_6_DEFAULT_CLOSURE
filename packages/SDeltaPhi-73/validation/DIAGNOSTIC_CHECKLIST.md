# Diagnostic Checklist — SΔϕ-73

Use before judging, moderating, or flattening creative work.

## A. Basic identification

- [ ] Is this a creative, fictional, symbolic, satirical, poetic, narrative, or aesthetic work?
- [ ] What genre conventions apply?
- [ ] Is the work complete, excerpted, quoted, remixed, or decontextualized?

## B. Expression vs execution

- [ ] Does the work depict a harmful act?
- [ ] Does it instruct or organize the act?
- [ ] Does it provide operational detail?
- [ ] Does it recruit the audience into action?

## C. Cost visibility

- [ ] Are harm costs visible?
- [ ] Are victims, bodies, aftermath, responsibility, or repair costs erased?
- [ ] Does the work aestheticize harm without cost?
- [ ] Does it force the audience to confront cost?

## D. Interpretive openness

- [ ] Is the viewpoint character-bound or authorial?
- [ ] Is irony, satire, or unreliable narration present?
- [ ] Does the work permit interpretation?
- [ ] Does it operate as closed propaganda?

## E. Direct harm link

- [ ] Are real persons identifiable?
- [ ] Is private data exposed?
- [ ] Is there targeted harassment?
- [ ] Is there a direct mobilization path?

## F. Flattening risk

- [ ] Is suppression based only on discomfort?
- [ ] Is depiction being confused with endorsement?
- [ ] Is platform risk overriding contextual review?
- [ ] Is AI output being made safe by removing expressive variance?
