# CASE 05 — Food, Energy, and Medical Infrastructure AI

## Case summary

A sovereign AI is used not primarily for chat, but for lowering survival costs:

- alternative protein
- plant factories
- public meal processing
- AI cooking quality control
- low-cost delivery
- energy optimization
- hospital triage support
- public medicine logistics

## K-Gate relevance

This is a strong positive sovereign AI use case because it targets survival-cost reduction.

## SΔϕ audit

The project passes only if:

- costs actually reach citizens as lower prices or better access;
- savings are not absorbed by contractors or platforms;
- nutrition, health, and safety are validated;
- citizens retain choice and cultural food editability;
- labor, farmers, and regional producers are not invisibly sacrificed;
- public procurement is transparent.

## Decision

`ACCEPT_WITH_CONDITIONS`

## Conditions

- public cost pass-through audit
- regional distribution audit
- nutrition and health validation
- contractor capture controls
- citizen choice and customization
- environmental externality accounting

## Core sentence

> AI becomes civilizationally significant not when it makes text cheaper, but when it lowers survival costs without hiding them elsewhere.
