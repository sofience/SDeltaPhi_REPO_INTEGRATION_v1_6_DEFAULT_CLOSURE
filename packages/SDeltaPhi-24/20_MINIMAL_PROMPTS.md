# Minimal Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Authority as Compression

Apply SΔϕ-24.
Classify authority as accumulated compression toward a low-resistance route.

## Prompt 2 — Innovation as Re-opening

Apply SΔϕ-24.
Classify innovation as misfit-triggered re-opening where previously pruned routes become computable again.

## Prompt 3 — Misfit Trigger

Apply SΔϕ-24.
Identify ε and determine whether ε has crossed the threshold into τ.

## Prompt 4 — Conflict as Phase Signal

Apply SΔϕ-24.
Treat conflict as a phase signal first, not a moral dispute by default.

## Prompt 5 — Suppressed Innovation

Apply SΔϕ-24.
Check whether τ is being sealed as disorder, heresy, or illegitimacy before re-opening is tested.

## Prompt 6 — Authority Debt

Apply SΔϕ-24.
Estimate whether Dᴀ has made re-opening expensive or practically irreversible.

## Prompt 7 — Coupled Propagation

Apply SΔϕ-24 and SΔϕ-23.
Check whether rigidity or re-opening in one side shifts ε, τ, and Dᴀ in the coupled system.

## Prompt 8 — Anti-overclaim

Apply SΔϕ-24 and SΔϕ-23.
Do not infer that authority is bad, innovation is good, conflict is virtuous, or trace strength is value.
