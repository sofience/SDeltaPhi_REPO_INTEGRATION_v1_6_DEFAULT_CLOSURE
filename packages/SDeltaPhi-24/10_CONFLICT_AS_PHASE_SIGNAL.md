# Conflict as Phase Signal

SΔϕ-24 defines authority-innovation conflict as a phase transition signal.

```text
conflict = τ
```

## Not moral dispute

Authority-innovation conflict is not primarily a moral disagreement.

It signals that a compressed route may no longer fit the environment.

## Two paths

```text
τ accepted → re-opening O → innovation

τ suppressed/reframed → suppressed innovation → closure precursor
```

## Boundary

Do not moralize conflict by default.

Do not treat conflict as automatic virtue.

Diagnose whether conflict is a re-opening trigger.
