# Boundary Notes

SΔϕ-24 excludes the following from its scope:

- moral theory of authority;
- romantic theory of innovation;
- business innovation theory by itself;
- political doctrine;
- claim that authority is bad;
- claim that innovation is always good;
- claim that conflict is automatically virtuous;
- justification for suppressing innovation as disorder.

This package fixes only minimal compression, misfit, re-opening, and authority-innovation phase dynamics.

## Negative constraints

Do not romanticize innovation by default.
