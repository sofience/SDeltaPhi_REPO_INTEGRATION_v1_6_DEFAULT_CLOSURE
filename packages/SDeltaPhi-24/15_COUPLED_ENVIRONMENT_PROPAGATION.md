# Coupled Environment Propagation

In co-environmentalization, authority-innovation conflict propagates across systems.

```text
CE: ε, τ, Dᴀ propagate across systems
```

## Meaning

Rigidity on one side can increase misfit in the other.

Re-opening on one side can destabilize or reopen route computation in the other.

## Governance problem

Authority-innovation conflict becomes a coupled governance problem rather than a private preference problem.

## Diagnostic question

Does one side's rigidity or re-opening shift the phase of the coupled system?
