# Minimal Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Reopening Audit

Apply SΔϕ-31. Determine whether a closed default has been operationally reopened or only symbolically ruptured.

## Prompt 2 — Trigger Recognition

Apply SΔϕ-31. Has the anomaly been recognized as a trigger rather than tolerated as background noise?

## Prompt 3 — Cost Assumption

Apply SΔϕ-31 and SΔϕ-56. Who assumes the cost of interrupting the cheapest continuation?

## Prompt 4 — Reopening Act

Apply SΔϕ-31. Has an excluded or suppressed alternative become operational again?

## Prompt 5 — Persistence of Re-entry

Apply SΔϕ-31. Does the re-entry path persist beyond the first rupture?

## Prompt 6 — Public Petition without Reopening

Apply SΔϕ-31 to a public petition case. Do not evaluate policy merits first. Test T_r, C_a, R_o, and P_r.
