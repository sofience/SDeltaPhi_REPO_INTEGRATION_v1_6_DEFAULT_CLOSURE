# Boundary Notes

SΔϕ-27 excludes the following from its scope:

- claim that efficiency is bad;
- claim that all routines are dangerous;
- claim that low cost is always fixation;
- claim that all stable paths must be disrupted;
- business efficiency theory by itself;
- institutional critique by itself;
- moral theory of authority;
- proof that successful systems are false.

This package fixes only minimal exception-compression, low-cost convergence, and path-fixation dynamics.

## Negative constraints

Do not confuse operational cheapness with reversibility.

Do not confuse smooth continuation with editability.

Do not treat success as proof of truth.

Do not treat efficiency as proof of universal adequacy.

Do not disrupt every routine by default; monitor editability first.
