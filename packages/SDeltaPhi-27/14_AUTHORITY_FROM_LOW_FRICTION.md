# Authority from Low Friction

SΔϕ-27 explains one generator of authority.

```text
low friction
-> repeated selection
-> path fixation
-> authority
```

## Meaning

Authority may emerge not from command, truth, or explicit legitimacy, but from repeated low-cost continuation.

## Failure

```text
low friction mistaken for truth
-> operational success mistaken for universal adequacy
-> authority hardens
```

## Boundary

Authority is not automatically false.

But authority can be generated by cost convergence rather than truth.
