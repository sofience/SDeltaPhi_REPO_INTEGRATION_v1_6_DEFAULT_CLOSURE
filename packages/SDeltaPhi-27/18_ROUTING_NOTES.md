## Call SΔϕ-27 when the task involves

- low-cost convergence;
- exception compression;
- operational cost;
- restoration cost;
- path fixation;
- repeated cheap continuation;
- alternatives becoming unaffordable;
- smooth routines becoming hard to revise;
- low friction hardening into authority;
- successful routine risk;
- editability monitoring.

## Do not call SΔϕ-27 merely because something is efficient

If the task asks about business efficiency, use domain-specific analysis.

If the task asks about irreversibility through restoration cost, route through SΔϕ-26.

If the task asks about authority editing, route through SΔϕ-22.

If the task asks about innovation as re-opening, route through SΔϕ-24.
