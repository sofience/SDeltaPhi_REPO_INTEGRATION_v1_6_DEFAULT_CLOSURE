# Efficiency as Hidden Fixation

SΔϕ-27 warns that efficiency can store future editing difficulty inside present operational ease.

```text
present operational ease
-> hidden future restoration cost
```

## Mechanism

The cheaper a path becomes in daily operation, the less frequently alternatives are exercised.

The less frequently alternatives are exercised, the more costly they become to recover.

## Core warning

```text
Efficiency carries a hidden temporal gradient.
```

## Boundary

Efficiency is not bad by default.

But efficiency without editability monitoring may become fixation.
