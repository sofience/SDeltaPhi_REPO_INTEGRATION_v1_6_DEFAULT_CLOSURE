# Editability Monitoring

SΔϕ-27 implies that editability must be monitored even during success.

## Monitor

```text
exception frequency E(P)
operational cost C_op(P)
repetition R(P)
fixation F(P)
restoration cost C_rest(P)
alternative route exercise frequency
```

## Core rule

```text
Monitor smooth routines before they become expensive to revise.
```

## Diagnostic

If alternatives are not exercised, restoration cost may be increasing silently.

## Boundary

Do not disrupt every routine.

Track whether low-cost convergence is preserving or destroying editability.
