# Minimal Examples

## Broken Cup

The prior configuration may remain physically describable.

But reconstructing the exact prior state may be prohibitively costly.

```text
formal describability != practical restorability
```

## Internet Trace

A post may be deleted.

But copies, screenshots, indexing, social propagation, and interpretive memory may remain.

```text
formal deletion != prior world-state restoration
```

## Virtual Rollback

A simulated environment may rewind.

But observers, logs, external systems, and branching consequences may retain the trace.

```text
local rollback != total condition restoration
```
