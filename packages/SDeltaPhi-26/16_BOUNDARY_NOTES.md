# Boundary Notes

SΔϕ-26 excludes the following from its scope:

- metaphysical finality theory;
- claim that reversal is logically impossible;
- claim that all changes are irreversible events;
- claim that digital rollback restores the total prior world-state;
- claim that deletion equals restoration;
- moral theory of consequence;
- legal finality doctrine;
- claim that all traces are fixed events.

This package fixes only minimal restoration-cost and event-fixation dynamics after pre-detection.

## Negative constraints

Do not treat formal reversibility as practical reversibility.

Do not treat local rollback as total restoration.

Do not treat deletion as restoration.

Do not treat all transitions as fixed events.

Classify restoration cost and practical return threshold first.
