# Appendix C — Certainty as Authority-Weighted Fixation

Certainty is a stabilized path granted authority. It increases coherence and speed but raises the cost of revision and exception re-entry.

## Illustrative uses

- A doctrine becomes treated as unquestionable.
- A regulatory interpretation becomes a non-editable default.
- An institutional model becomes the only legitimate framing.

## Diagnostic caution

Certainty is not automatically irrational. Its risk lies in the collapse of editable exception channels.
