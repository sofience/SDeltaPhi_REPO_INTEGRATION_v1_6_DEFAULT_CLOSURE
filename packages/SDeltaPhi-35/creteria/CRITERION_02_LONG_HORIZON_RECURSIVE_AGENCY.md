# Long-Horizon Recursive Agency

## Definition

Long-Horizon Recursive Agency is the ability to preserve and update goal-directed structure across extended sequences rather than merely producing competent local responses.

## Closure condition

Goal(t) remains coherent through Goal(t+k) and path continuation remains stable under interruption pressure.

## Failure mode if open

appears agentic only in short bursts; can begin but not sustain.

## Evaluation note

Evaluate this condition under heterogeneous stress, not merely in curated demonstrations.
