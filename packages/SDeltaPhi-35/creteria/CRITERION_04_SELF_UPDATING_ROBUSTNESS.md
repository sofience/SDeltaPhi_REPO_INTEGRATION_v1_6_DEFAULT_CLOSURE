# Self-Updating Robustness

## Definition

Self-Updating Robustness is the ability to incorporate new information and constraints without catastrophic degradation of structural coherence.

## Closure condition

Meaningful update occurs while pattern persistence remains above threshold and self-damage remains below instability bound.

## Failure mode if open

changes and degrades, or preserves itself by refusing genuine adaptation.

## Evaluation note

Evaluate this condition under heterogeneous stress, not merely in curated demonstrations.
